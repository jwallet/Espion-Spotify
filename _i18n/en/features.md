## File features
- Splits the recorded sound into individual tracks using the artist and track names as the title, like so:
   - `Artist - Track.mp3`
- Saves all recordings under the same path:
   - `../My Music/`
- Automatically adds metadata to .mp3 file:
   - \# track
   - Track Title
   - Artist
   - Album Title
   - Album Art Cover
   - Genre
